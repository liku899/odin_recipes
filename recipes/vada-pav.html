vada pav

Description

Vada Pav is a popular street food from Mumbai, consisting of a spicy potato fritter placed inside a bun and served with chutneys and fried green chilies.

Ingredients

  • 3 medium potatoes
  • 2 green chilies
  • 4 pav buns
  • oil
  • spices
  • chutney

Steps

  1. Saut√© mustard, cumin, ginger, garlic, green chilies, and curry leaves in hot oil.
  2. Add turmeric and mashed potatoes, mix well with salt, and cook for a minute.
  3. Let the mixture cool, then shape it into small round balls.
  4. Prepare a thick batter by mixing gram flour, turmeric, salt, baking soda, and water.
  5. Dip each potato ball in the batter and deep fry until golden; serve inside pav with chutney and fried chilies.
